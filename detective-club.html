About this game

Welcome to the Detective Club! Led by the troublesome and shy president Alice Baskerville. Follow this comedy story where together you will solve cases using your deductive powers!

A normal school with a strange rule... Due to a certain situation, you are the new member of the Detective Club. Alongside the scatterbrained Alice Baskerville, you'll have to solve your first case as a detective! The future of the club is in your hands! Using logical deduction, you'll be able to connect the evidence to formulate hypotheses about what really happened. Ask the right questions to identify inconsistencies in the testimonies and solve puzzles that challenge your deductive reasoning!

Features

  • A short and charismatic comedy story, 2 hours of gameplay.
  • Puzzle mini-games.
  • Make interrogations, ask the right questions and find the inconsistencies in the testimonies.
  • A notepad where you will be able to see all the collected evidences.
  • A debate with everyone involved, choose your best argument to affirm or deny the accusations.
  • A story with many references to Sherlock Holmes!
